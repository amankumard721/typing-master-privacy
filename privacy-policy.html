Typed Text

KrutiDev Keyboard is an Android input method. The app converts key presses locally on the device into Hindi text. The app does not collect, store, upload, sell, share, or analyze typed text.

Ads

The app may use Google Mobile Ads SDK to show banner ads. Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share ad-related data such as IP address, user product interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. Typed text is not sent to the ads SDK by this app.

Network Access

Internet and network-state permissions are used to load ads when ads are enabled. The keyboard typing conversion itself works locally on the device.

Local Settings

Theme, sound, vibration, and related keyboard preferences may be stored locally on the user's device. These local settings are not uploaded by the app.

Children

The app is not designed for children and is intended as a general productivity/input utility.

Security

The app is designed to keep typed text local. Data handled by Google Mobile Ads SDK is governed by Google's SDK and advertising policies and is encrypted in transit according to Google Mobile Ads SDK documentation.

Data Retention and Deletion

The developer does not maintain user accounts or typed-text records for this app. Users can clear app data from Android settings to remove local preferences. Users can reset or delete their Android advertising ID from Android privacy settings. Privacy requests can be sent to the contact email above.

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page.
